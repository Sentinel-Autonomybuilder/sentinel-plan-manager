dVPN Plan Manager
Build and operate a subscription dVPN service on Sentinel. Create plans, pick your nodes, and connect any payment method — all from one console.
  • 1Create a plan — set your price and duration, pick the nodes, and share it with your users.
  • 2Accept any payment method — take card, fiat, or crypto through Stripe, PayPal, or your own checkout. Your users never have to touch a token.
  • 3Cover network fees — pick up the tab for your subscribers so they can connect with zero friction.
  • 4Scriptable for automation — everything in this console is also available as a CLI, so you can automate onboarding, bulk updates, and renewals from your own tools.
Import your wallet
Paste the 12 or 24 word mnemonic of the wallet that will own this plan. The phrase is held in server memory only — never written to disk.

Create a new account

Back up your recovery phrase
Write these 24 words down in order. Store them somewhere safe and offline. This is the only way to recover your wallet.
